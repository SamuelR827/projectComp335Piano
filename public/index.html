Welcome to our COMP 335 Final Project!

This is the final project of Samuel Rantz and Amber Chaves. We have created an interactive piano that you can either click the keys with your mouse or using your computer's keyboard. The symbols on the piano show the corresponding key to press on your computer keyboard. The piano starts on a C3 and contains two octaves. The chart below also shows which keys correlate to which note. Enjoy and play whatever you would like!
